Artists That Inspire Me

Nathan Fowkes

Nathan Fowkes is a gouache painter and concept artist known for his breathtaking landscapes and expressive style. He inspires me because his paintings are truly mesmerizing and full of depth.

Alison Aletha

Alison Aletha is an illustrator who brings stories to life through her cozy and heartfelt gouache paintings. I get inspired by her art and try to capture the same warmth and storytelling in my own work.

Hayao Miyazaki

Hayao Miyazaki is a legendary animator and filmmaker whose creativity and style have shaped the world of animation. I look at his movies and art as inspiration for my own work, admiring the detail, emotion, and magic he creates.
