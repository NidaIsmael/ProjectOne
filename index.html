About me

Whether through painting or writing, Nida enjoys using art to tell stories and capture feelings. She believes creativity is not just about making something beautiful but also about uniquely sharing ideas and perspectives. Nida finds inspiration in nature, books, and everyday experiences. She especially enjoys painting landscapes with gouache, as it allows her to bring the beauty of nature to life with color and detail. Reading also inspires her, and she hopes to explore writing as another creative outlet in the future. Classic literature is one of her favorites, but she also enjoys fantasy and romance novels. Her main goal is to build a career in digital marketing. She wants to work in branding, social media, and content creation, helping businesses connect with their audiences. She hopes to use both data and creativity to make marketing more engaging and effective. Marketing excites her because it blends strategy and storytelling, allowing her to create meaningful campaigns that resonate with people. Born and raised in the Bay Area, Nida has always had a mix of cultures shaping her identity. With Yemeni roots, she values the traditions and experiences that have been passed down in her family. These influences have shaped her perspectives and the way she approaches creativity and storytelling. Growing up in a diverse environment has given her an appreciation for different viewpoints and ways of life. It has also strengthened her creativity and adaptability. After graduating, Nida hopes to work in digital marketing, focusing on brand storytelling and creative strategy. She is excited to apply her skills in a professional setting and continue learning and growing in the industry. She is passionate about creativity in all forms—whether it’s marketing, painting, reading, or writing. She loves bringing ideas to life and finding new ways to connect with people through art and storytelling.
